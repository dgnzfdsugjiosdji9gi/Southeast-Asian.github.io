Purpose of doing this project

The purpose of this Southeast Asia education project is to spread awareness about the severe educational disparities in the region, highlighting issues such as lack of resources, outdated curricula, and limited access to quality education. By shedding light on these challenges, we aim to inspire action, advocate for policy changes, and promote innovative solutions to improve learning opportunities for all students.



Essential Question

How can we enhance education systems in Southeast Asia by sharing institutional best practices and exploring educational opportunities for students?



Driving Question

How can we create a website that highlights key standards, provides reliable resources, and promotes lasting benefits for our community?
